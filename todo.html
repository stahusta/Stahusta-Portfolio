Polish Butcher Shop

La Maialata is an Italian butcher shop, It deals of production and transformation of the pork meat. For La Maialata we created a modern identity of American inspiration, for this reason we adopted specific typographic and illustration solutions. We chosen a specific color palette and a vermillion red to recall the meat color and the classic sign of the italian butchers shops. We designed the logo, business card, sign, aprol, flyer and shopping bags.
